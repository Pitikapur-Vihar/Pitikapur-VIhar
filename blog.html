Pithapuram Padagaya - Why It Is a Sacred Place?

Pithapuram is home to **Padagaya Kshetram**, one of the holiest places for Hindus. It is believed that performing rituals here brings peace to ancestors. Thousands of devotees visit **Kukkuteswara Swamy Temple** and **Sri Puruhutika Shakti Peetham** for blessings.
